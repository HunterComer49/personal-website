Innovative System Resume Upload & Manager Websites

This project was created using Angular and a C# RESTful API Backend. The objective of this project was to decrease the amount of time that Innovative System's employees spend to collect information from possible hires and give them a quick and easy way to view all the uploaded applications. These websites will be first implemented Fall 2019 at the South Dakota School of Mines & Technology Career Fair. Here, they will give the website url to applicants, rather than having to collect many resumes and ask for information later in the hiring process. This will also give them a way to keep the history of who applied in the past years. Before these websites, the company was just using excel spreadsheets in order to keep track of past applicants, the scheduling of interviews, and whether or not they have offered them a job and if they accepted the offer or not.
